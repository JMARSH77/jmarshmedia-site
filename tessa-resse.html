About Tessa.

The Author

“I’m Tessa. I write stories about women who swear, drink, and fall for men who don’t scare easy.”

Tessa Reese writes for the woman who knows the difference between a fairy tale and a fighting chance. Her work—whether it's a raw journal or a full-throttle romance novel—is confident, sensual, and brutally honest about what love truly costs. She believes in chaos, laughter, and that the best stories happen when the filter comes off.

The Philosophy

“I don’t write fairy tales. I write what happens after the wedding, the kids, the bills, and the silence.”

Her books and prompts are designed for readers who've lived long enough to stop apologizing for their past, and are ready to demand more from their future. If you appreciate candlelight, chaos, and a dirty martini, you're in the right place.
