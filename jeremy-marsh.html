The Songwriter's Heart

Jeremy's narrative approach isn't limited to the page. As a songwriter, he explores the same themes of character, place, and hidden conflict through concise, emotional lyrics. His music is often described as **Kentucky Folk-Rock**, combining earnest storytelling with a driving, roots-inspired sound.
